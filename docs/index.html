Small Business Websites, Done Right

We started Holy Smokas specifically for small businesses who need a professional online presence without getting trapped in expensive monthly contracts or complex hosting arrangements.

Most small businesses don't need fancy cloud infrastructure - you just need a great-looking, fast website that shows up on Google. That's why our standard packages include free hosting on reliable infrastructure. You pay once for the website, own it forever, and never worry about hosting bills.

For businesses that do need more - like full e-commerce platforms with shopping carts and inventory management, or enterprise solutions with advanced features - we've got you covered too. We'll build on cloud infrastructure with transparent, usage-based pricing. No surprises, no hidden fees.
